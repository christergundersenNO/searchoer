The idea behind OER Global Search is to make it easy for anyone to find learning resources that supports reuse, re-mixing and re-contextualization. We have included some of the best OERs like Khan Academy, CK-12, OER Commons, MIT OpenCourseware. The complete list of all the projects and sites inluded in the search.

Most of the resources are in English but we have included resources in many other languages like Hindi, French, Spanish and Norwegian. You will even find some resources in Swahili and Amharic.

If you want to take it a step further and make your own OER thats asome! We have made a list of greate tools that you can use to create your own Open educational resource. You can get started in minutes, the only thing you need is a computer, tablet or a smart phone.

If a learning resource is released with Copyright you can not reuse or change it. That is why a free lisence is so important for any Open educational resourse. If your are new to the term OER you should take a few minutes and learn more about Creative Commons.

Creative Commons helps you legally share your knowledge and creativity to build a more equitable, accessible, and innovative world. Free licenses unlock the full potential of the internet to drive a new era of development, growth and productivity.

If you are looking for other resources like pictures and videos that are released under a Creative Commons license you shold check out this services at Creativecommons.org.
